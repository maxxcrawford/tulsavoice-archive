GALLERY: Smashing Pumpkins at Brady Theater

12/2/2018

By Phil Clarkin


Smashing Pumpkins perform at Brady Theater with vocal accompaniment from Hanson

Smashing Pumpkins headlined 7104.5’s Christmas Concert on Dec. 2, with support from The Nixons and Kitten. Billy Corgan and co.’s set included fan favorites, such as “1979,” “Cherub Rock,” “Tonight, Tonight,” and “Bullet With Butterfly Wings,” as well as some surprising covers and guest appearances from Oklahoma musicians. Hanson joined the band for a take on Fleetwood Mac’s “Landslide,” and Chainsaw Kittens singer Tyson Meade sang on David Bowie’s “Suffragette City.” The Pumpkins also performede Depeche Mode’s “Never Let Me Down Again” and The Cure’s “Friday I’m In Love.”

Setlist:

  1. “Solara”
  2. “Zero”
  3. “Today”
  4. “Never Let Me Down Again”
  5. “Knights of Malta”
  6. “Tonight, Tonight”
  7. “Dross”
  8. “Friday I’m In Love”
  9. “Landslide” w/ Hanson
  10. “Disarm”
  11. “Bullet With Butterfly Wings”
  12. “Porcelina of the Vast Oceans”
  13. “Suffragette City” w/ Tyson Meade
  14. “Travels”
  15. “Cherub Rock”
  16. “1979”
  17. “Ava Adore”
  18. “Heavy Metal Machine”
  19. “Silvery Sometimes (Ghosts)”
  20. “Muzzle”
